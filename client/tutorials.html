How to Download YouTube Videos

5 min read • Beginner Friendly

GrabBlitz supports YouTube videos in resolutions from 144p up to 4K (2160p), as well as audio-only MP3 and M4A extraction.

Step 1: Find Your YouTube Video

Open YouTube and navigate to the video you want to download. You can download any public video, playlist, or YouTube Short.

Step 2: Copy the Video URL

Copy the URL from the browser address bar. It should look like: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xxxxxxxxxx

Step 3: Paste into GrabBlitz

Head back to GrabBlitz's homepage, paste the URL into the input box, and click the Download button.

Step 4: Choose Your Quality

GrabBlitz will display all available video and audio formats. Choose your preferred quality:

  • 2160p (4K) – Best quality, large file size
  • 1080p Full HD – Great quality for most screens
  • 720p HD – Balanced quality and size
  • Audio MP3 – Music and podcast extraction

Step 5: Download

Click the Download button on your chosen format and the file will save directly to your device. That's it!

?? Tip: For YouTube Shorts, simply paste the Shorts URL — GrabBlitz handles them the same way as regular videos.

Save TikTok Videos Without Watermark

3 min read • Beginner Friendly

Step 1: Open the TikTok Video

Find the TikTok video you want to save. Open it in the TikTok app or web browser.

Step 2: Share & Copy Link

Tap the Share button on the video and select Copy Link. The URL will look like: https://vm.tiktok.com/xxxxxxx/

Step 3: Paste in GrabBlitz

Go to GrabBlitz, paste the link and hit Download. GrabBlitz will automatically detect TikTok and fetch the watermark-free version.

Step 4: Download

Choose Video (HD) from the format list and click Download. The clean, watermark-free video will save to your device.

?? Tip: GrabBlitz removes TikTok watermarks automatically. You don't need to do anything extra!

Download Instagram Reels & Stories

4 min read

Downloading Instagram Reels

Open Instagram and find the Reel you want. Tap the three-dot menu (?) and select Copy Link.

Paste the link into GrabBlitz and click Download. Select the video quality and save!

Downloading Instagram Stories

For Stories, open the story in your browser (via Instagram web) and copy the URL. Paste it into GrabBlitz.

Downloading Posts & Carousels

For regular posts, tap the three-dot menu and select Copy Link. GrabBlitz will download individual posts or all images from a carousel.

?? Note: Private account content can only be downloaded if you are logged in. Public content works without login.

3 min read

Step 1: Find the Tweet

Open Twitter/X and find the tweet containing the video or GIF you want to save.

Extract MP3 Audio from Any Video

3 min read

Extract Audio in 3 Easy Steps

GrabBlitz can extract audio from any supported video platform — including YouTube, SoundCloud, TikTok, and more.

  1. Copy the URL of the video whose audio you want
  2. Paste it into GrabBlitz and click Download
  3. Switch to the Audio tab in the results and click the MP3 download button

Supported Audio Formats

  • MP3 – Universal compatibility (128kbps, 320kbps)
  • M4A – High quality AAC audio
  • OGG – Open source format
  • OPUS – Modern, efficient codec
?? Pro Tip: For music downloads, always choose 320kbps MP3 for the best listening experience.
