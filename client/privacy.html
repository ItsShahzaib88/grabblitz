Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2025 • Effective from: July 1, 2025

TL;DR: We don't collect personal data, we don't store your URLs, and we don't sell anything to anyone. GrabBlitz is built with your privacy as the top priority.

1. Introduction

Welcome to GrabBlitz ("we", "our", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website at GrabBlitz.com and our media downloading service.

By using GrabBlitz, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you disagree, please discontinue use of the service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

GrabBlitz does not require account creation. We do not collect:

  • Names or email addresses (unless you contact us)
  • Payment information
  • Profile data

If you contact us via our contact form, we collect your name, email address, and message content to respond to you.

2.2 Automatically Collected Data

Like most websites, we automatically collect some technical data:

  • Log data: IP address (anonymized), browser type, pages visited, time of visit
  • Cookies: Only essential cookies for theme preferences (dark/light mode)
  • Analytics: Anonymized usage statistics via privacy-respecting tools

2.3 URL Data

When you paste a URL to download, it is sent to our servers to process the download. We do not store or log these URLs after the request is complete. URLs are processed in real-time and immediately discarded.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the limited data we collect to:

  • Provide and improve the GrabBlitz service
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Monitor server performance and prevent abuse
  • Analyze anonymized usage trends

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share limited data with:

  • Hosting providers who operate our infrastructure (under strict data processing agreements)
  • Law enforcement if required by valid legal process

5. Cookies

GrabBlitz uses minimal cookies:

  • gb-theme — Stores your dark/light mode preference (localStorage, not a network cookie)

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies that share data with advertisers.

6. Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions for up to 90 days for support purposes, then securely delete them. Server access logs are retained for 30 days for security monitoring.

7. Your Rights (GDPR & Privacy Rights)

If you are located in the European Union or similar jurisdictions, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to processing
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@GrabBlitz.com.

8. Children's Privacy

GrabBlitz is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

9. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, server firewalls, and regular security audits. However, no internet service can guarantee 100% security.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We'll post the new policy on this page with an updated date. Continued use of GrabBlitz after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

11. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@GrabBlitz.com
  • Contact Form: GrabBlitz.com/contact
