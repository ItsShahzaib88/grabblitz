About GrabBlitz

Built by a developer who was tired of slow, ad-filled, unreliable download tools.

Our Story

GrabBlitz was born out of frustration. In 2024, our founder Shahzaib needed a simple way to download tutorial videos for offline viewing during trips with no internet. Every tool he tried was either filled with ads, required software installation, had rate limits, or simply didn't work.

So he built GrabBlitz — a clean, fast, and completely free media downloader that respects users' time and privacy. What started as a weekend project quickly grew into a tool used by millions of people worldwide.

"I wanted a tool that just works. No ads, no sign-ups, no slowness. That's exactly what GrabBlitz is." — Shahzaib, Founder

Today GrabBlitz supports 30+ platforms, handles millions of downloads every month, and continues to grow with new features and platform support added regularly.
